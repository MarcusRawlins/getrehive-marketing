# ReHive SEO Research Notes

Date: 2026-04-23

## Research framing

The goal for the first version of `getrehive.com` is to rank around commercial-intent queries where ReHive can win as a custom development partner for small businesses, especially operators who have outgrown generic website builders, booking systems, and disconnected software.

This first-pass research uses publicly accessible search-demand references and SERP-visible keyword datasets. Exact monthly volume should still be validated later in Google Search Console and a paid keyword platform, but the demand direction is already clear enough to drive page architecture now.

## Highest-priority commercial themes

### 1. Custom software and custom development

- `custom software development` — 8,100 avg. monthly searches, commercial intent, CPC `22.66` ([Media Search Group](https://www.mediasearchgroup.com/industries/seo-keyword-ideas-for-it-companies.php))
- `software development company` — 8,100 avg. monthly searches, informational/commercial intent, CPC `39.46` ([Media Search Group](https://www.mediasearchgroup.com/industries/seo-keyword-ideas-for-it-companies.php))
- `software development agency` — 5,400 avg. monthly searches, commercial intent, CPC `39.46` ([Media Search Group](https://www.mediasearchgroup.com/industries/seo-keyword-ideas-for-it-companies.php))
- `software development services` — 4,400 avg. monthly searches, commercial intent, CPC `33.65` ([Media Search Group](https://www.mediasearchgroup.com/industries/seo-keyword-ideas-for-it-companies.php))
- `custom software development company` — 3,600 avg. monthly searches, commercial intent, CPC `25.31` ([Media Search Group](https://www.mediasearchgroup.com/industries/seo-keyword-ideas-for-it-companies.php))

Takeaway: these terms are valuable and expensive, which makes them strong positioning targets for core service pages even if they are competitive.

### 2. Small-business website and service-business site demand

- Public SERPs repeatedly surface `web design services near me`, `small business website designer [city]`, and `service business website` as local-intent phrases tied to Google Business Profile visibility and local-pack behavior ([Small Business Web Design](https://smallbusinesswebdesign.co/), [Website Forces](https://websiteforces.com/our-services/service-business-web-design/))
- `ecommerce website design` — 5,400 avg. monthly searches, informational/commercial intent, CPC `8.40` ([Media Search Group](https://www.mediasearchgroup.com/industries/seo-keyword-ideas-for-ecommerce.php))
- `ecommerce agency` — 5,400 avg. monthly searches, informational/commercial intent, CPC `14.29` ([Media Search Group](https://www.mediasearchgroup.com/industries/seo-keyword-ideas-for-ecommerce.php))
- `ecommerce platform` — 6,600 avg. monthly searches ([Media Search Group](https://www.mediasearchgroup.com/industries/seo-keyword-ideas-for-ecommerce.php))
- `ecommerce website` — 9,900 avg. monthly searches ([Media Search Group](https://www.mediasearchgroup.com/industries/seo-keyword-ideas-for-ecommerce.php))

Takeaway: ReHive should not only target broad dev terms. It also needs pages built around high-converting site/redesign language that small business owners actually use when they are shopping.

### 5. Lawn care and landscaping demand

The strongest publicly accessible result here was directional rather than a fully text-readable table. iNET Web documents that landscaping companies in Milwaukee are driven by service-plus-location keyword combinations and recommends specialized city/service targeting over broad terms ([iNET Web](https://www.inet-web.com/IndustriesDominated/Landscaping)).

Takeaway: lawn care should stay in the initial ReHive positioning and should get its own vertical page, but we should tighten exact volume with a dedicated keyword tool during the next SEO pass.

### 6. Food truck demand

Public search results surface meaningful demand around high-intent food truck discovery and catering queries:

- `food trucks` — 450,000 monthly searches ([Sona](https://www.sona.com/blog/google-ads-for-food-trucks-step-by-step))
- `food trucks near me` — 450,000 monthly searches ([Sona](https://www.sona.com/blog/google-ads-for-food-trucks-step-by-step))
- `food truck catering` — 14,800 monthly searches ([Sona](https://www.sona.com/blog/google-ads-for-food-trucks-step-by-step))

Takeaway: food trucks are worth targeting, but the best ReHive angle is not broad discovery. It is the operational and conversion layer: schedule/location updates, catering inquiries, local visibility, and brand trust.

## Recommended page architecture

### Core commercial pages

- Homepage: target `custom development for small businesses`, `small business web development`, `custom software development for small business`
- `/custom-web-development`: target website-build and redesign intent
- `/automation-and-workflows`: target automation and process-improvement intent
- `/custom-software-for-service-businesses`: target custom software and operational-system intent

### Vertical pages

- `/mobile-detailing-websites`
- `/cleaning-company-websites`
- `/lawn-care-websites`
- `/food-truck-websites`
- `/ecommerce-development`

### Supporting SEO pages

- `/booking-platform-customization`
- `/service-business-seo-websites`
- `/small-business-website-redesign`
- city/service templates later once target markets are defined

## Copy direction

- Lead with the gap: most software solves 50-90% of the business problem
- Sell outcomes first: save time, reduce admin work, increase visibility, improve conversions, strengthen the brand
- Keep “custom” and “affordable” in tension: premium outcomes without enterprise overhead
- Speak to operators, not tech buyers

## Next SEO tasks

- Validate exact US search volume in a paid keyword tool for the lawn-care and service-business head terms
- Build dedicated metadata and copy targets for each vertical page
- Add long-tail supporting content around pricing, platform limitations, automation, and workflow pain points
- Connect the domain to Search Console at launch so the content roadmap starts using first-party query data fast
